My Story

Hello! I'm Swarnil Singhai, a software engineer and content creator based in Bangalore, India. I specialize in frontend development with a focus on React and Next.js, but I'm also experienced in full-stack development.

My journey in tech began during my university years, where I discovered my passion for web development. After graduating with a degree in Computer Science, I started my career as a Junior Developer at a startup, where I honed my skills in JavaScript and frontend development.

In 2016, I launched my YouTube channel to share my knowledge and experiences with the developer community. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a platform with over 500,000 subscribers. I create tutorials, project walkthroughs, and tech reviews to help developers at all stages of their careers.

Currently, I work as a Senior Software Engineer at TechCorp Inc., where I lead frontend development for enterprise applications. I'm also dedicated to creating comprehensive online courses and producing high-quality content for my YouTube channel.

When I'm not coding or creating content, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying outdoor activities like hiking and photography.
